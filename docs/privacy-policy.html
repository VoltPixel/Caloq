Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

VoltPixel ("we", "us", "our") operates the Caloq mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Account Data (optional)

Creating an account is entirely optional. If you choose to sign in, we collect:

DataPurposeStorage
Email addressAccount identification, password resetFirebase Auth (Google)
Google Account name and profile pictureGoogle Sign-In onlyFirebase Auth (Google)
User ID (anonymous token)Linking recipe ratings to a userFirestore (Google)

The app works fully offline without an account. No account data is required for core features.

1.2 Recipe Ratings (optional)

If you are signed in and rate a recipe, we store your user ID, your rating (1–10), and the timestamp in Cloud Firestore. Ratings are anonymous — no name or email is associated with individual ratings.

1.3 Local Data (on-device only)

The following data is stored exclusively on your device and is never sent to our servers:

  • User profile (calorie targets, dietary preferences)
  • Meal plans and daily food logs
  • Weight history
  • Shopping lists
  • Favourite recipes and custom recipes

1.4 Health Data (Android Health Connect)

With your explicit permission, Caloq can read and write data to/from Android Health Connect:

  • Nutrition — writing calorie/macro data from your meal logs
  • Weight — reading and writing body weight measurements

This data stays on your device within Health Connect and is never transmitted to our servers.

1.5 Device Permissions

PermissionUsed for
CameraBarcode scanner (food product scanning)
MicrophoneVoice search for recipes
NotificationsMeal reminders and hydration alerts (optional)

All permissions are optional. You can deny them and still use the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To display community recipe ratings (aggregated, anonymous)
  • To enable account features (sign-in, sync of ratings)
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising purposes

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

4. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen in the app. Upon deletion, all account-related data (email, ratings) is permanently removed from Firebase within 30 days.

Local data can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

5. Children's Privacy

Caloq is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures. Firebase services are protected by Google's security infrastructure. Local data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database on your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

1.2 Oceny przepisów (opcjonalne)

Jeśli jesteś zalogowany/a i ocenisz przepis, przechowujemy Twoje ID użytkownika, ocenę (1–10) oraz datę w Cloud Firestore. Oceny są anonimowe — żadne imię ani e-mail nie są powiązane z konkretną oceną.

1.3 Dane lokalne (tylko na urządzeniu)

Następujące dane są przechowywane wyłącznie na Twoim urządzeniu i nigdy nie są wysyłane na nasze serwery:

  • Profil użytkownika (cele kaloryczne, preferencje dietetyczne)
  • Plany posiłków i dzienniki żywieniowe
  • Historia wagi
  • Listy zakupów
  • Ulubione przepisy i własne przepisy

1.4 Dane zdrowotne (Android Health Connect)

Za Twoją wyraźną zgodą Caloq może odczytywać i zapisywać dane w Android Health Connect:

  • Żywienie — zapisywanie danych o kaloriach/makroskładnikach z dziennika posiłków
  • Waga — odczyt i zapis pomiarów masy ciała

Dane te pozostają na Twoim urządzeniu w Health Connect i nigdy nie są przesyłane na nasze serwery.
